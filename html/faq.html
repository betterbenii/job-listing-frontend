Frequently Asked Questions

To apply for a job, simply click the "Apply Now" button on the job details page. You will be redirected to an application form.
You can register by clicking the "Sign Up" button on the top right corner of the homepage. Choose either "Candidate" or "Recruiter" during registration.
Once you submit an application, the recruiter will review your profile and contact you if you are shortlisted.
If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to us through the "Contact Us" page.
To delete your account, go to your profile settings and click on "Delete Account." Please note that this action is irreversible.
